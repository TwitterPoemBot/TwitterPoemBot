Twitter is one of the most prominent social networks today with a huge amount of data posted to it every minute. Although the data sets are large, there seems to be limited usages for it outside of the actual Twitter website. Our project, Twitter Poem Generator, seeks to make use this data to generate several types of poems based on specific hashtags.
